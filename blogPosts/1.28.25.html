Our Idea!

Posted on January 28, 2025

My dad, Joey, and I have always been interested in projects. Particularly, projects that involve creating something that holds virtually no documentation thereof. We got to talking one day and started discussing his 1978 Ford E150 5.8L black Shag Van of which he has no use for. What better than to repurpose it into an electric vehicle? After discussing the project, we decided to make this a publicly-funded and heavily influenced community project. We will use our website and other Social Media outlets to continue to post updates on how our project is going, what worked and what didn't work as well as accepting donations to fund the project. Thank you all in advance for your support, we can't wait to get started!

Collin Brooks
